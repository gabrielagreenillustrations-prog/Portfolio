# AI Simulator for Conversational Flows
## Testing AI-driven conversations before going live

---

## Overview

The AI Simulator was designed as a feature within AtomChat to test the effectiveness of conversational flows before deploying them to real users.

AtomChat is an omnichannel SaaS platform that enables businesses to communicate with customers through channels like WhatsApp using automated conversational flows.

---

## Problem

Before this feature:

- Testing was manual and inefficient
- Teams had to simulate conversations themselves
- No structured insights or metrics were generated
- High dependency on support and onboarding teams

---

## Strategy

Introduce an AI-powered simulation system to:

- Automate conversation testing
- Generate realistic scenarios
- Provide structured insights

---

## Key Decisions

- Allow configuration of simulations (flow, volume, scenario type)
- Enable personality-based AI responses
- Add customer profile inputs (age, tone, intent)
- Support training via files or past conversations
- Design flexible outputs for AI variability

---

## UX Challenges

- Designing for unpredictable AI outputs
- Creating a scalable results interface
- Balancing flexibility with structure

---

## Iterations

| Version | Scope |
